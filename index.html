(C)ONCOCT

Content Creation

Overview

This is an event that provides participants a platform to showcase the brightest minds from various schools. Our aim is to create an unparalleled platform for collaboration, innovation, and skill development by designing an Instagram page.

Eligibility Criteria

For classes 9th-12th

Participation

The team shall consist of 3 members: videographer, video editor & graphic designer.

Duration

The team should complete all the tasks under the time limit of 2.5 hours.

Rules and Regulations

  • The team has to prepare an Instagram page consisting of 5 posts (3 graphic posts and 2 reels).
  • The theme/topic of the page will be given on the spot. The judgment will be based on the creative outlook.
  • Participants should bring their own equipment (lights, laptop, camera, pen drive etc.)
  • INTERNET WILL BE PROVIDED
  • The team cannot use templates to create reels.

Prizes

1st position: Cash prize of Rs. 2500

2nd position: Cash prize of Rs. 1500

3rd position: Cash prize of Rs. 1000

(C)IFI

Videography || VideoEditing

Overview

This event combines both videography and video-editing skills of the teams. A topic/product will be given on the spot. Each team will have to create an advertisement for it.

Eligibility Criteria

For classes 9th-12th

Participation

Both the videography and video-editing teams should have only 2 members each.

Duration

The combined team should complete their task within the given time limit of 90 minutes for editing and 30 minutes for shooting.

Rules and Regulations

  • The videography and video-editing teams will be divided into two.
  • The team will not be allowed to leave the school premises.
  • The topic will be given on the spot to individual teams.
  • Participants are to bring their own equipment (laptop, camera, pendrive etc.)

Prizes

1st position: Cash prize of Rs. 2500

2nd position: Cash prize of Rs. 1500

3rd position: Cash prize of Rs. 1000

(C)ROSSHAIR

Overview

Embraced by computer gamers of all ages, Valorant stands as a pinnacle of gaming excellence. This upcoming event offers an unparalleled opportunity for both current and future generations of gamers to showcase their prowess in this acclaimed title. It serves as a rigorous test of coordination, teamwork, individual capabilities, and strategic acumen, pushing participants to their limits. The tournament promises to be a spectacle of skill and determination, with teams vying to outwit and outmaneuver their opponents through stunning displays of gameplay and tactical mastery. Ultimately, only the most resilient and skilled team will emerge victorious, claiming the coveted grand prize.

Eligibility Criteria

For classes 8th-12th

Participation

Team of 5+1(substitute) per school

Duration

The team should complete all the tasks under the time limit of 2.5 hours.

Rules and Regulations

  • The competition will begin with all the teams divided into 2 groups.
  • These teams will be then put through a deathmatch; only one member per team will be allowed in the deathmatch.
  • The 5v5 matches will start once the top 4 teams have been decided through the results of deathmatches
  • These 5v5 matches will follow the standard ranked Valorant rules.
  • The semi-finals and finals will be broadcasted live on YouTube on the official (c)ypher channel.

Prizes

1st position: Cash prize of Rs. 6000

2nd position: Cash prize of Rs. 4000

3rd position: Cash prize of Rs. 2500
